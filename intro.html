Accessing the Calendar

To access the calendar, simply click on the "Calendar" link in the navigation menu at the top of any page. This will take you to the main calendar view where you can see your monthly schedule and manage your tasks.

You can also navigate directly to the calendar page by visiting calendar.html

Switching Between Dark and Light Mode

CalendarPro supports both dark and light modes to suit your preferences and reduce eye strain. To switch between modes, click on the moon/sun icon in the top right corner of any page.

  • Click the moon icon to switch to dark mode
  • Click the sun icon to switch back to light mode
  • Your preference will be saved for future visits

Calendar Features

Monthly Calendar View

The monthly calendar view displays the current month with all your scheduled tasks and deadlines. The current day is highlighted for easy reference.

  • Navigate between months using the previous/next buttons
  • Click on a date to view or add tasks for that specific day
  • Tasks and deadlines are color-coded for easy identification
  • Hover over a task to see a brief preview of its details

Adding Tasks

To add a new task, click on the "Add Task" button below the monthly calendar. Fill in the required details such as task name, start time, end time, location, and description.

  • Select the task type (Lecture, Tutorial, Training, etc.)
  • Set the start and end times for the task
  • Add a location if the task is in-person
  • Include additional details in the description field
  • Add a link to relevant resources if needed

Add New Task

Adding Deadlines

Add New Deadline

To add a new deadline, click on the "Add Deadline" button below the monthly calendar. Fill in the required details such as deadline name, due date, and description.

  • Set the due date and time for the deadline
  • Add a description to provide more context
  • Include a link to relevant resources if needed
  • Deadlines will be highlighted on the calendar
  • Upcoming deadlines will be shown in the today's summary

Daily Schedule View

The daily schedule view displays your tasks for the selected day in a timeline format, with each hour represented. This allows you to see your day at a glance and easily manage your time.

  • Tasks are displayed in chronological order
  • Each task shows the time, title, and type
  • Click on a task to view or edit its details
  • Drag and drop tasks to adjust their time
  • Scroll through the timeline to see the entire day

The today's summary section displays all tasks and deadlines for the selected day. This gives you a quick overview of what you need to accomplish for the day.

  • Tasks are grouped by type and color-coded
  • Each task shows the title, time, and location
  • Deadlines are displayed separately with due times

Task Types

Lecture

Formal educational sessions where information is presented to a group of students.

Tutorial

Small group sessions focused on discussion, problem-solving, and hands-on learning.

Training

Structured learning activities to develop specific skills or knowledge.

Meeting

Gatherings for discussion, decision-making, or information sharing with colleagues.

Assignment

Academic tasks or projects that need to be completed by a specific deadline.

Exam

Formal assessments to evaluate knowledge or skills in a particular subject.

Break

Scheduled time for rest, relaxation, or personal activities.

Other

Any other type of task that doesn't fit into the above categories.

Frequently Asked Questions
